About Me

My names Vinnie, I'm a recent BSc Biology graduate at Newcastle University (2023) persuiting a career in Software Engineering. Why persuit a career in tech and not science?
In my final year at Newcastle University, I conducted a reasearch project invesigating food preferences in birds using Deep Learning. This project sparked my interest in programming and the real world applications of it.
With very basic knowledge of programming let alone artificial intelligence, I spent 6 months gathering data and building an image classification model to assist in my research.
The project was a huge success with the Image Classification saving me hours by using the image classification model to identify species of birds rather than manually identifying species.
Post-Graduation I continued to work at NatWest Bank as a personal banker whilst I pondered what my long term aspirations were. It wasn't until I took a sebattical to backpack Asia/Oceania where I realised that I wanted a career in programming, meeting so many travellers who work as Software Engineers, Data Analysts and Web Developers whilst travelling inspired me to want to do the same.

Below you will can some personal projects that I have embarked on to hone my programming skills.
